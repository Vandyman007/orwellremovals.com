Flexible Help When You Need It

Moving out of a flat, shifting furniture to a relative, clearing a single room or collecting a marketplace purchase? Our man and van service handles it without the expense of booking a full removal team. We arrive with blankets, straps and trolleys, take care to protect both your belongings and your property, and work through the job efficiently so you are not left waiting around for half a day.

Because every booking is different, we keep things flexible. Tell us roughly what needs shifting and where it is going, and we will match the right van and the right number of hands to the task. Whether it is one trip across Ipswich or a couple of loads further afield, you only pay for the time and vehicle you actually need, with no padding added on.

What we can help with

Single items and small furniture, studio and one-bed moves, student moves at the end of term, eBay and marketplace collections, tip runs of unwanted furniture, and those awkward jobs that are simply too big for the car yet too small to warrant a lorry. A man and van is often the sensible middle ground when a friend with an estate car just will not cut it.

We regularly help customers who have bought a sofa, wardrobe or appliance and have no way to bring it home, as well as those downsizing a few rooms at a time. If you are genuinely unsure whether you need a man and van or a full home removal, just ask and we will advise honestly, even when that means recommending the cheaper option.

Add packing or storage

Short of time before the move? We can supply sturdy boxes and materials, or take the job off your hands entirely with our packing service, wrapping fragile items properly so nothing rattles loose in transit. It is a small addition that often saves a stressful evening of last-minute boxing up the night before.

Need somewhere to put things for a while between addresses, or while you sort out a new place? Our clean, secure storage is available by the week or the month, for however long you need, with no pressure to commit to a long contract. Drop a few boxes in or store a whole room's worth; the choice is yours, and your goods stay fully covered throughout.

Across the county

Our man and van covers Ipswich and the wider county, taking in Felixstowe, Woodbridge, Stowmarket and the villages in between. As a family-run firm based here in Suffolk since 2005, we know the narrow lanes, parking quirks and one-way systems that can catch out drivers from further away, which keeps each job moving smoothly.

If your collection or delivery sits just outside these towns, it is still worth asking, as we travel across the region regularly and can often fit smaller jobs around larger bookings. To get a clear idea of timings and cost for your particular move, simply get a quote and we will talk you through exactly what is involved.
