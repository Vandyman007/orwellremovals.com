Built for Business Moves of Every Size

Commercial removals are rarely just “furniture from A to B”. There is stock to inventory, equipment to protect, IT to disconnect carefully, and a deadline that usually can’t slip. As a family-run firm working across Suffolk since 2005, we begin with a proper site survey, agree a written plan and timetable, and confirm exactly who needs to be on site and when so nothing is left to chance.

From there we bring the right number of trained crew and the right vehicles to finish inside the window you have, whether that means an evening, a weekend, or a tightly phased weekday move. Every job is fully covered, and we keep one point of contact throughout so your facilities team or office manager always knows what is happening next. Tell us your priorities and we build the schedule around them.

Stock, fixtures and equipment

We move shelving, display units, retail stock, catering and gym equipment, machinery, server racks and heavy filing as part of everyday commercial removals. Fragile or high-value items are wrapped, padded and crated as needed, and we label and inventory each load so it can be checked off cleanly at the far end. Bulky or awkward kit is assessed beforehand so the right trolleys, straps and lifting help are ready on the day.

Where downtime matters, we can phase the move so part of your operation keeps trading while the rest relocates — tills and front-of-house first, back-office and storeroom after hours, for example. We also coordinate with your own fitters or IT contractors so cabling, racking and shelving are taken down and rebuilt in the right order, keeping the whole project moving without surprises.

Storage and staged relocations

Not everything needs to arrive at once, and a clean handover often depends on having somewhere to park the overflow. Our business storage in Ipswich lets you stage a move, hold surplus stock, or keep fixtures between sites — clean, dry and secure at our Westerfield base, with access arranged to suit your trading hours rather than ours.

This is especially useful when a lease ends before the new premises are ready, or when a refit means furniture and equipment must come out for a few weeks. We can collect, store and redeliver in stages, so you only take what you need when the new space is genuinely ready. For a tailored plan and pricing, simply request a quote and we will map it out with you.

Local, and further afield

We cover commercial premises throughout Suffolk, from Felixstowe’s port businesses to high streets in Sudbury, Stowmarket and across Ipswich itself. Knowing the area well means we plan realistic timings around loading restrictions, one-way systems and parking, so your crew aren’t left waiting and the move stays on schedule from the first box to the last.

When a relocation reaches beyond the county, our long-distance removals team handles business moves the length of the country with the same care and planning.
