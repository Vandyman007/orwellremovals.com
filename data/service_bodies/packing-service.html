Fragile and valuable items

Glassware, china, framed pictures, mirrors and electronics all get extra attention, individually wrapped and cushioned so they survive loading, transit and the bumps of an unfamiliar driveway. For especially delicate or high-value pieces we can crate or double-box, and we will always flag anything we feel needs special handling before it goes anywhere near the van.

Larger or more awkward valuables sometimes call for specialist help, and we are happy to advise on the safest method for each one. If you own an instrument that needs more than careful wrapping, our piano removals team can take that on, while every other fragile item stays firmly within the packing service so nothing slips through the gaps.

Materials to buy, and unpacking too

Prefer to pack yourself? We can supply strong, removal-grade boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper and tape so you are never relying on flimsy supermarket cartons that collapse halfway up the stairs. We will happily suggest sensible quantities for your size of home, so you order enough to finish the job without a last-minute dash for more materials the night before.

And if you would like a hand at the other end, our packing service runs in reverse too: we can unpack, place your belongings where you want them and take all the empty boxes and used wrapping away. That means you settle into your new home far faster, without a hallway full of flattened cardboard waiting to be dealt with for weeks.
