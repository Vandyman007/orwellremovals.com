Packing for the distance

On a long distance move your belongings travel hundreds of miles rather than a few streets, so how they are packed makes all the difference. Our professional packing service wraps fragile items individually, fills boxes so nothing rattles loose, and labels each one by room for a smooth unload. We bring sturdy materials and pack methodically, giving your possessions the best protection for a long motorway journey.

Furniture gets the same care and attention. We blanket-wrap sofas, beds, wardrobes and tables, then secure everything firmly inside the vehicle so it cannot shift as the miles tick by. Sharp corners are padded, mirrors and glass are boxed, and heavier pieces are positioned low and braced. It is the kind of preparation that keeps your home arriving in the same condition it left.

Flexible timing and storage

Long property chains and long distances rarely line up perfectly, and a delayed completion should never leave your belongings stranded. If your new home is not ready on the day you move out, our secure storage in Ipswich holds everything safely for as long as you need, from a few nights to several months, with no pressure to rush the next stage of your move.

When the keys are finally yours, we load up and deliver to your new address at a time that suits you, completing the door-to-door journey without you having to lift a thing. We talk all of this through openly when we prepare your quote, setting out the storage and delivery options clearly so you can plan your relocation with genuine confidence.
