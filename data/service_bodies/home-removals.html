Packing and dismantling

You are welcome to pack yourself to keep costs down, and we are happy to drop off sturdy boxes and materials in advance so you can work at your own pace. Or you can hand the whole job to us with our full packing service, where our trained team carefully wraps everything from glassware and pictures to wardrobes and electricals, labelling as we go so nothing gets lost in the shuffle.

Furniture is rarely as simple as carrying it out of the door, which is why dismantling and reassembly is part of what we do. Our crew will take apart beds, wardrobes, flat-pack units and garden furniture, keep all the fixings safe and organised, then rebuild everything in your new home. That means you are not left hunting for an allen key or wrestling with a half-built bed frame on moving night — you can simply unpack and settle in.

If there is an awkward gap between moving out of one home and getting the keys to the next, you should not be left with a loaded van and nowhere to put it. That is exactly the situation our flexible service is built to handle, without panic or extra stress on what is already a busy day.

Our clean, dry, secure storage at our Westerfield base bridges that gap neatly. We can hold your belongings for a few nights or several weeks, whatever the chain demands, and deliver them as soon as your new home is ready. Everything is carefully inventoried and kept under cover, so a delayed completion never derails your plans or leaves your possessions sitting in a driveway overnight.
