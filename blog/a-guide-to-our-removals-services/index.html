A Guide to Our Removals & Storage Services

Get a Free Quote 01473 411531

Moving Guides · Orwell Removals & Storage

People often ask what we actually do beyond turning up with a van, and the honest answer is rather more than that. As a family-run firm working out of Westerfield since 2005, we've built our services around the real situations Suffolk people find themselves in, from a single-room flat move to a full house relocation with storage in the middle. This guide walks through everything we offer, so you know exactly what help is on hand when you come to move.

Home Removals, Big and Small

The heart of what we do is moving people from one home to another, and after two decades we've handled just about every variation. A young couple leaving their first flat, a growing family upsizing across Ipswich, a retired pair downsizing to a coastal bungalow; each move is different, and we size the crew and the vehicle to fit. There is no point sending a huge lorry to a one-bed flat or a small van to a four-bedroom house, so we always match the job properly from the start.

Whatever the size, the approach is the same: careful, methodical and friendly. Our crews protect your floors and doorways, wrap furniture so it travels safely, and load with the experience that only comes from doing it day in and day out. We treat your belongings as we would our own, because in a family firm your reputation walks out of the door with every move, and we want every customer to feel they were in safe hands.

We also handle the trickier homes that put others off. Narrow Suffolk lanes, thatched cottages with low doorways, townhouses with no parking and upstairs flats with awkward stairs are all part of the job for us. Tell us about the access at both ends when you book, and we will bring the right vehicle and plan the right approach so the day runs smoothly.

Packing Services to Suit You

Packing is the part of moving people most underestimate, and it is where a good service makes a real difference. We offer a full packing service, where our crew comes in and carefully boxes up your entire home, and a partial service, where we tackle the awkward or fragile items and leave the rest to you. Plenty of customers like us to handle the kitchen, the glassware and the pictures while they manage their own clothes and books, and we are happy to mix and match.

The advantage of professional packing goes beyond saving you time. Properly packed boxes load faster, stack better in the van and arrive in far better condition. We use the right materials for each item, double-walled boxes for heavy things, plenty of paper and bubble wrap for breakables, and wardrobe boxes so clothes travel on their hangers. Everything is labelled by room so unpacking at the other end is straightforward rather than a guessing game.

For anyone short on time or simply daunted by the task, the packing service is often the single thing that turns a stressful move into a calm one. We genuinely enjoy it too, because a well-packed home is a pleasure to load, and we know it will arrive safely. If you would rather pack yourself, we can also supply the materials so you have everything you need to do the job well.

Storage Solutions for Every Situation

Storage runs right through what we do, because so many moves are not a simple door-to-door affair on a single day. Completion dates slip, chains leave gaps, families downsize and need somewhere to keep furniture, and businesses need space for stock and archives. We've helped countless Suffolk customers bridge these in-between moments by storing their belongings securely until the time is right to deliver them.

Our storage suits a wide range of needs. Short-term storage covers the awkward few days or weeks when a moving chain does not line up perfectly, letting you complete your move without panic. Longer-term storage works for people renovating, spending time abroad, or simply not ready to bring everything into a new, smaller home. And for businesses, we store stock, equipment and records so their premises stay productive. Everything is kept secure and is fully covered while it is with us.

The real benefit of having storage and removals under one roof is that we plan it all as one smooth operation. Rather than juggling a separate storage firm and a separate mover, your belongings are collected, stored and delivered by the same team who knows your move inside out. In our experience, that joined-up approach takes a great deal of pressure off, especially when a move is not running quite to plan.

Surveys, Quotes and Honest Advice

Every good move starts with an accurate quote, and an accurate quote starts with someone actually understanding what is being moved. That is why we offer a proper survey, either in person or by video walk-through, rather than guessing a price from a postcode. A survey lets us see the wardrobe that needs dismantling, the piano in the front room, the loft that is fuller than you remember, and the access at both ends, so the price we give you reflects reality.

We believe in being straight with people. Our quotes are in writing and set out clearly what is and is not included, so there are no awkward surprises on the day. If a survey throws up something that will affect the cost or the plan, we tell you there and then rather than springing it on you later. We would far rather have an honest conversation up front than leave you wondering, and we think that openness is a big part of why customers come back to us and recommend us.

The survey is also your chance to ask anything. How many crew will come, what protection we provide, whether dismantling and reassembly are included, how we handle delays in the chain; ask it all, and we will answer plainly. A firm that welcomes your questions is one you can trust, and we always want you to feel fully informed before you commit to anything.

Dismantling, Reassembly and the Extra Touches

A removal is more than carrying boxes, and it is the extra touches that make a move feel properly looked after. Our crews dismantle the furniture that needs it, beds, wardrobes, flat-pack units and the like, at the old house, and reassemble it at the new one, so you are not left wrestling with an Allen key at midnight surrounded by parts. We keep the fixings safe and labelled so nothing goes missing between the two ends.

We handle the awkward and the precious with particular care. Heavy and delicate items like pianos, large mirrors, antiques, glass cabinets and bulky white goods all need the right technique and the right protection, and that is exactly the experience a seasoned crew brings. We protect your new home as we carry things in, laying down coverings and easing furniture through doorways rather than risking a knock to a freshly painted wall.

It is the small considerations that customers remember. Placing boxes in their labelled rooms so unpacking is simple, taking care over a treasured piece of furniture, a friendly word on a stressful morning, and a final check round the old house so nothing is left behind. These things do not appear as line items on a quote, but in our experience they are what turn a satisfactory move into one people are genuinely glad they chose us for.

Why Choose a Family-Run Suffolk Firm

There is a real difference between a faceless national operation and a family-run local firm, and it shows on moving day. Working out of Westerfield since 2005, we know Suffolk intimately, from the A12 and A14 traffic patterns to the village lanes a large lorry cannot manage and the parking quirks of Ipswich's older streets. That local knowledge means fewer surprises and smoother days, because we have almost certainly handled a move like yours nearby before.

Being family-run also shapes how we treat people. Our name and our reputation are tied to every job, so we cannot afford to be anything other than careful, honest and friendly. The crews who come to your home are part of a settled, experienced team rather than a rotating cast of agency staff, and that consistency shows in the quality and the care. When you call us, you are dealing with people who genuinely want your move to go well.

Above all, we aim to make a stressful day feel manageable. Moving home or premises is one of life's bigger upheavals, and the right firm makes all the difference. Whether you need a simple single-van move, a full pack-and-store relocation, or business storage that flexes with your year, we bring the same combination of local expertise, careful hands and straightforward advice that has kept Suffolk people coming back to us for the best part of two decades.
